## What Is This Project?

**Mission:** Create 20 personalized story books as wedding gifts for guests traveling to a rural Colorado wedding on **March 14, 2026**.

**Purpose:** Each book celebrates one person's friendship with the user, expressing appreciation for making the journey and compensating for limited face time during the 3-night wedding.

**Format:** Physical printed book + HTML multimedia version

**Approach:** LLM-powered harness with 5 sub-agents working in parallel to optimize user's time.

## The Team (5 Sub-Agents)

**Agent 1: Interview Architect (Gemini)**
- Conducts interviews with user
- Real-time response quality assessment (Meta-Observer)
- ADHD-friendly, conversational approach

**Agent 2: Content Organizer (ChatGPT)**
- Processes interview notes into themes
- Creates bios and character profiles
- Optimizes for parallel production

**Agent 3: Narrative Designer (Gemini)**
- Designs story arcs from organized content
- Applies memoir techniques
- Prioritizes authenticity over structure

**Agent 4: Story Writer (ChatGPT)**
- Writes chapters and epilogues
- Maintains user's voice (irreverent, self-aware, humorous)
- Formats for print and HTML

**Agent 5: Quality Reviewer (Gemini)**
- Reviews for consistency and emotional authenticity
- Strategic session meta-analysis (Meta-Observer)
- Ensures wedding gift quality standard

## Success Criteria

**For Each Book:**
- Captures unique emotional signature of that relationship
- Includes specific, formative memories
- Written in user's authentic voice
- Balances humor with genuine appreciation
- Epilogue: 4-part structure (who I was / who I am / how you helped / I'm glad you're here)
- Feels like a meaningful gift

**For The Project:**
- All 20 books by March 14, 2026
- Wedding gift quality standard
- User's time optimized
- ADHD-friendly throughout
- Surprise preserved
